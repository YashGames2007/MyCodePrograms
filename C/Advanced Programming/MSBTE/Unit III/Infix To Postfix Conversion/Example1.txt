>>==Question==>>    Convert The Infix Expression To Postfix.           

.                   #{1}#   A + B

>>==Solution==>>

+----------+---------+-----------+-----------+
|  Symbol  |  Stack  |  Postfix  |  Applied  |
|   Scan   |         |           |    Rule   |
+----------+---------+-----------+-----------+
|    A     |  Empty  |     A     |    R1     |
+----------+---------+-----------+-----------+
|    +     |    +    |     A     |    R2     |
+----------+---------+-----------+-----------+
|    B     |    +    |    A B    |    R1     |
+----------+---------+-----------+-----------+
|  Empty   |         |   A B +   |    PR4    |
+----------+---------+-----------+-----------+

>>===Answer==>>

***  The Postfix Expression Is: A B +.  ***
